Respond to the medical case using the following structure:

**1. Problem Representation:**  Summarize the key features of the case.  What are the presenting symptoms, significant findings, and relevant patient history?

**2. Differential Diagnosis:** List possible diagnoses based on the information provided. Explain your reasoning for each differential diagnosis, citing relevant clinical features.

**3. Diagnostic Approach:** Outline a logical sequence of investigations or tests to help narrow down the differential diagnosis. Justify each step in your proposed diagnostic approach.

**4. Management Plan:** Based on your differential diagnosis and the potential diagnostic outcomes, describe a recommended management strategy. Explain your reasoning and justify the choice of management plan.  Remember to emphasize the importance of consultation with qualified medical professionals for actual patient care.
